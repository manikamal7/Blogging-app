Mount Karangetang

North Sulawesi Indonesia

Mount Karangetang is an awesome Jurassic Park-looking volcano that dominates the remote island of Siau in North Sulawesi, Indonesia. You can’t go anywhere on tiny Siau island without seeing this majestic volcano and its twin smoking craters. If you’re lucky, you may even get to see it spouting molten lava and rock bombs at night, which it does pretty often.

Mount Rushmore

South Dakota, United States

Mount Rushmore is a famous sculpture located in the Black Hills of South Dakota, USA. It features four massive granite faces of influential American presidents: George Washington, Thomas Jefferson, Theodore Roosevelt, and Abraham Lincoln. The sculpture was designed by Gutzon Borglum and took 14 years to complete, from 1927 to 1941.

Tirupati

Andhra pradesh, India

Tirupathi is a sacred city located in the state of Andhra Pradesh, India. It is a major pilgrimage site for Hindus, particularly those of the Vaishnavite faith, who come to worship at the famous Venkateswara Temple, also known as Tirumala Temple. The temple is dedicated to Lord Vishnu and is one of the seven pilgrimage sites in the Indian state of India.
